Common Questions

If you don't see your answer here, reach out — we respond within hours.

What exactly is OpenClaw?+
OpenClaw is an AI agent platform that connects to your existing tools — email, calendar, Slack, CRM — and automates workflows. Unlike ChatGPT, it actually does things on your behalf: sends emails, schedules meetings, generates reports, routes leads.
How long does setup take?+
Most deployments are live within 48 hours. Enterprise setups with custom skill development may take 3–5 business days.
Do I need technical skills to use it?+
None. We handle all the technical setup. Your training call covers everything you need to interact with your agent day-to-day — it's as easy as sending a message.
Is my data safe?+
Yes. OpenClaw runs on your own infrastructure. We configure it, hand you the keys, and your data never touches our servers. You own everything.
What if I need changes after launch?+
All packages include a support window. For ongoing tweaks, our monthly retainer ($150/mo) gives you priority access for new automations, adjustments, and troubleshooting.
Can I upgrade later?+
Absolutely. Upgrade anytime — you only pay the difference between tiers.
